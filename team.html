BECACO

An interdisciplinary team of archaeologists, museum studies scholars, and data scientists — working alongside a network of curators across 9 countries to advance cultural heritage provenance research.

Project Director · PI
Martin Berger

Dr. Martin E. Berger is Assistant Professor of Museums, Collections, and Society at Leiden University, the Netherlands. His research focuses on the development of the market for ‘pre-Columbian art’. Prior to joining Leiden University he was a curator of the Latin American collections at the Dutch National Museum of World Cultures (now ‘Wereldmuseum’)

He leads the overall research design of BECACO and manages partner relationships across 9 countries.
