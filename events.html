Latest Event
● 2026 Workshop Paper BECACO Output
SemDH 2026 @ ESWC — Generation of Multi-Modal Narratives of Cultural Objects from Knowledge Graphs and LLMs

BECACO presents a full-team paper at the Third International Workshop on Semantic Digital Humanities, co-located with ESWC 2026 in Dubrovnik. The work introduces an automated pipeline that transforms structured museum data into a CIDOC CRM knowledge graph, queries it with Cypher to extract per-object context, feeds it to an LLM to generate coherent historical narratives, and converts the output into audio for multi-modal accessibility. Validated by both domain experts and general museum audiences.

📍 Dubrovnik, Croatia — ESWC 2026 Conference Venue
👥 Authors: Ishak Riali, Raphaël Gersen, Elizabeth Rodriguez Estrada, Gabriel Spautz Vieira, Martin Berger
